QRIS Payment Guide

  1. Inform the partner that you wish to pay with QRIS.
  2. The partner will show you a QR code.
  3. Open your preferred banking or e-wallet application (e.g., GoPay, OVO, Dana, M-BCA).
  4. Scan the QR code provided by the partner.
  5. Enter the correct payment amount and confirm the transaction with your PIN.
  6. Show the successful transaction proof to the partner.
